About Budget

We are greatly excited with the new enthusiasm to inspire beginners in Data Science. Nepal Data Awareness is self motivated philanthrophic movement where interested people can study, discuss and move forward by following our training syllabus. Each topics in training syllabus are designed with recomended free lectures from online courses form cursera, edx, udacity etc. as well as informative tutorials and assignment projects. This is a self pased syllabus. You can study as much as you pay your time. You can update your progress in google sheet and your github repo (if you want but not mandatory).

We are using slack as our discussion platform which will help you to solve your problem from your peers. Since there are more then two hundred participants, it is not possible to track everybody's progress but we do have automated progress report tracker designed in google script using your progress sheet. Once you feel confident with the subject matter, you can participate in our interesting projects based on open and simulated data. These projects are design just for learning purpose with our slogan learn and rock!

Data Science is growing to become a huge demand in comming future. Almost all sectors for example Technology, Entertainment, Education, Healthcare, Business etc are evolving with new ideas in Artificial Intelligence(AI), BigData, Virtual Reality(VR), Internet of Thing (IoT), Blockchain, Quantum Computing, Personalized Medicine etc.

If you are a beginner in this field and wondering how to start with, Nepal Data Awareness could be your starting platform to get introduced with Data Science. Nepal Data Awareness is a mission to teach more and more people about data science in national, social and economic domain.All data being used in our projects are open data and simulated data. We will put all our processed data and model open to the public. We are not any school, collage or institution and also not responsible for any data security.
